NO. This is prop money for display and entertainment purposes only. It is NOT legal tender and cannot be used as real currency. All bills are clearly marked as prop money.

Where can it be used? ▼

Our prop money is designed for entertainment, display, and creative use only. It should never be used to attempt to pass as real currency.

Our prop money features ultra-realistic printing with authentic textures and details. While it looks realistic, all bills include clear markings that distinguish them from genuine currency.
